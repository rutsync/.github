# RutSync

RutSync is an organization focused on academic routine management for universities and higher education institutions.

Our work is centered on building systems that help institutions manage, publish, and maintain class schedules with greater clarity, consistency, and administrative control.

## Focus

- Centralize academic routine data across departments, batches, semesters, and sections.
- Convert spreadsheet-based schedules into structured, searchable digital records.
- Improve routine access for students and faculty through organized presentation and practical filtering.
- Support administrative oversight through controlled updates, announcements, and role-based access.
- Preserve schedule integrity through traceable changes and standardized publishing workflows.

## Purpose

RutSync exists to improve how academic routine information is managed and delivered.

Rather than relying on scattered files, repeated manual revisions, and inconsistent copies of schedule data, we aim to provide institutions with dependable workflows and a single trusted source of schedule information.

## Current Direction

Our current work is centered on:

- routine access for public and authenticated users
- structured import and management of schedule data
- role-based access for academic and administrative stakeholders
- announcement delivery for routine-related communication
- audit-friendly history for administrative transparency

## Principles

- Institutional clarity over unnecessary complexity
- Reliable routine data as an operational priority
- Accessible information for students, faculty, and staff
- Consistent workflows across departments and user roles
- Practical systems aligned with academic operations
